== Description ==

YourTraffix it a plugin that helping you to get more traffix to your website by sharing your content on other websites.
How it works, By enabling yourtraffix feed you will start to share other people website posts on your website.
and your posts will start to appear on other website as well.


Your content is the best:

A website’s content is arguably the most important factor influencing conversion rates. It also plays an integral role in how your individual pages perform in organic search. Great marketing content has the ability to seamlessly blend conversion opportunities.


Get Free Backlinks:

Google and other major search engines consider backlinks “votes” for a specific page. Pages with a high number of backlinks tend to have high organic search engine rankings.
When you share other people's posts on your website, your posts will appear on other websites feeds as well, you will start getting backlinks and improve your ranking on search engines.


Share great content:

The contents we expose through the YourTraffix discovery platform is what’s interesting and new. It can be news, topical information or advice, a new product or service, or something entertaining.




== Installation ==

1. Upload `yourtraffix.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Enable YourTraffix feed on your website
1. choose posts that you want to share from your websites posts
1. start sharing



== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How it works? =

Right after enabling YourTraffix plugin you will start to appear on other people websites.

= What is the best content to share ? =

The best method to get more engagement and more attention is to share original content from your posts.

= What is TraffixIn/TraffixOut? =

TraffixIn represent the income traffic from other websites to your website.
TraffixOut represent the outcome traffic from your website to other websites.

= Does YourTraffix free? =

Every website have every month 30 free credits and can get free 60 users to the website.
